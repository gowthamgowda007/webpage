Swing of Cricket Ball using CFD and Machine learning

Description: This project investigates the aerodynamics of a cricket ball by Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) and machine learning. The objective is to predict the side forces acting on the ball during its flight, enhancing our understanding of its behavior in real-game scenarios. Objectives: CFD Simulation: Perform high-fidelity simulations to analyze airflow patterns around a cricket ball. Data Analysis: Collect and interpret aerodynamic data to identify critical factors affecting performance. Machine Learning Model Development: Create predictive models using CFD data to estimate side forces on the ball.
